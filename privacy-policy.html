Privacy Policy

Effective date: 26 April 2026
Last updated: 26 April 2026

🔒 Short version: Ringmatch stores game data locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. No accounts. No tracking. No ads.

1. Who We Are

Ringmatch is developed and published by Madness Games, based in Rotterdam, the Netherlands. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at mndlmadness@gmail.com.

2. What Data We Store

Ringmatch stores the following data locally on your device only using the app's local storage:

  • Your best scores and highest level reached in Classic and Casual mode
  • Your power-up inventory (including any purchased via Google Play)
  • Daily challenge completion status and consecutive day streak
  • Game preferences (sound on/off, haptics on/off, color theme)
  • Whether you have completed the tutorial
  • Anonymous gameplay statistics: total sessions started, tiles placed, rings completed, and games played per mode
  • Whether you have accepted this privacy policy

3. What We Do Not Collect

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Your device's unique identifier (IDFA, GAID, or similar)
  • Your location or IP address
  • Any browsing history or behavior outside the app
  • Payment information of any kind (handled entirely by Google Play)
  • Health or biometric data
  • Photos, contacts, or any other device content

4. Data Transmission

No gameplay data from Ringmatch is ever transmitted off your device. All game storage is local. We do not operate servers that receive gameplay data. We do not use any third-party analytics services such as Firebase, Amplitude, or Mixpanel.

5. In-App Purchases

Ringmatch offers optional in-app purchases (power-up packs) processed entirely and securely through Google Play. We do not handle, store, or have access to your payment details at any point. All billing is governed by the Google Play Terms of Service and Google's Privacy Policy.

In-app purchases are non-refundable except where required by applicable law. For refund requests, please contact Google Play support directly.

6. Third-Party Services

Ringmatch may load fonts from Google Fonts when an internet connection is available, subject to Google's Privacy Policy. Fonts fall back to system fonts when offline — no game functionality requires an internet connection.

The app does not contain advertisements and does not use advertising SDKs.

7. Children's Privacy

Ringmatch does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Because we collect no personal data whatsoever, the app is suitable for general audiences. If you have concerns, contact us at mndlmadness@gmail.com.

8. Data Deletion

All data stored by Ringmatch can be deleted at any time by:

  • Clearing the app's data: device Settings → Apps → Ringmatch → Clear Data
  • Uninstalling the app

We have no server-side copy of your data, so deletion from your device is complete and immediate.

9. Security

Since all data remains on your device and nothing is transmitted, the security of your game data is governed by your device's own security model. We recommend keeping your device software up to date.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the effective date above and, where appropriate, showing a notice within the app. Continued use of Ringmatch after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data under laws such as GDPR (EU/EEA), CCPA (California), or similar legislation. Since we collect no personal data, these rights are inherently satisfied — there is nothing to access, correct, or delete beyond what is already under your direct control on your own device.

For users in the EU/EEA: as a Netherlands-based developer, we are subject to GDPR. Our legal basis for storing local game data is legitimate interest in providing core game functionality. You may contact us at any time at mndlmadness@gmail.com with privacy concerns.
