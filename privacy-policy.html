2. What Data We Store

Ringmatch stores the following data locally on your device only using the browser's or app's local storage:

  • Your best scores and highest level reached
  • Your power-up inventory
  • Game preferences (sound on/off, haptics on/off, color theme)
  • Whether you have completed the tutorial
  • Anonymous gameplay statistics: total sessions started, tiles placed, rings completed, and games played per mode
  • Whether you have accepted this privacy policy

3. What We Do Not Collect

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Your device's unique identifier (IDFA, GAID, or similar)
  • Your location or IP address
  • Any browsing history or behavior outside the app
  • Payment information of any kind
  • Health or biometric data
  • Photos, contacts, or any other device content

4. Data Transmission

No data from Ringmatch is ever transmitted off your device. All storage is local. We do not operate servers that receive gameplay data. We do not use any third-party analytics services (such as Firebase, Amplitude, or Mixpanel).

5. Third-Party Services

Ringmatch may load fonts from Google Fonts when an internet connection is available. This is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. Fonts fall back to system fonts when offline — no game functionality requires an internet connection.

The app does not contain advertisements and does not use advertising SDKs.

6. Children's Privacy

Ringmatch does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Because we collect no personal data whatsoever, the app is suitable for general audiences. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will investigate — though our architecture makes this technically impossible by design.

7. Data Deletion

All data stored by Ringmatch can be deleted at any time by:

  • Clearing the app's data through your device's Settings → Apps → Ringmatch → Clear Data
  • Uninstalling the app

We have no server-side copy of your data, so deletion from your device is complete and immediate.

8. Security

Since all data remains on your device and nothing is transmitted, the security of your game data is governed by your device's own security model. We recommend keeping your device software up to date.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the effective date above and, where appropriate, showing a notice within the app. Continued use of Ringmatch after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data under laws such as GDPR (EU), CCPA (California), or similar. Since we collect no personal data, these rights are inherently satisfied — there is nothing to access, correct, or delete beyond what is already under your direct control on your own device.
